Shipping & Editions

Editions & Authenticity

Every image is released as a limited edition of 50 across all sizes combined. Each print is accompanied by a hand-signed, numbered certificate of authenticity, mailed separately from the print itself. When the 50th print sells, the edition closes and the image is permanently retired from sale.

The Prints

Prints are produced on ChromaLuxe aluminum with a high-gloss finish — dye infused directly into the coated metal for depth, luminosity, and archival stability no paper can match. Each panel arrives ready to hang with a float-mount backing that holds the print off the wall.

Shipping

Every print ships insured, in rigid art packaging, within the continental US at no additional cost. Sizes 36" and above ship freight with signature on delivery. Allow 2–3 weeks from order confirmation — each piece is printed, inspected, and signed to order. International shipping is available by quote — send your destination through the contact form and I'll price it within 24 hours.

Damage

If your print arrives damaged, photograph the packaging and the print and email me within 48 hours of delivery. I will produce and ship a replacement with the same edition number at no cost. The damaged print is destroyed — the edition count is never expanded.

Returns

If a print isn't right in your space, you may return it within 14 days of delivery for a full refund of the purchase price. The print must arrive back undamaged in its original packaging; return shipping is the buyer's responsibility. Returned edition numbers are re-released.

Privacy

The inquiry form collects your name, email, and message solely to respond to your inquiry and fulfill orders. Submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy). Your information is never sold or shared for marketing. To have your data deleted, email any time.
